Berzerker Legion is an international death metal band formed in 2016 by members of Hypocrisy, Asphyx, Dark Funeral and Vader. Combining elements of old-school brutality and solid harmonies, they quickly gained recognition for their intense sound and relentless energy. Their debut album, Obliterate the Weak, was released in 2020 and showcased their raw, uncompromising approach to death metal.

In 2023, the band followed up with their second album, Chaos Will Reign, further refining their sound with heavier riffs and intricate compositions. The album received strong praise from the metal community and solidified their place in the extreme music scene.

With the addition of new member Harris Sopovic in 2024, Berzerker Legion continues to push their boundaries while staying true to their roots. They remain committed to delivering powerful performances and uncompromising death metal to audiences worldwide.
